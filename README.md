# Pacific Science Center Food Map

An interactive map of walkable food venues within ~1 mile of the Pacific Science Center in Seattle, WA.

## Features

- 🗺️ Interactive map with 67+ food venues
- 🏷️ Filter by cuisine type (Pizza, Sushi, Seafood, Italian, Thai, and more)
- 📍 Color-coded pins by food category
- 📊 Category statistics and distribution charts
- ⭐ Gold star marker for Pacific Science Center
- 🎨 Pacific Science Center brand colors and typography

## How to Use

1. Open `food_map.html` in your web browser
2. Use the sidebar filters to explore restaurants by cuisine type
3. Click on any restaurant pin to see details
4. Scroll down to view all venues in a searchable table

## Walk Times

- **Seattle Center** (0-2 min walk): Armory vendors, PacSci Café
- **Center Edge** (2-5 min walk): Zeeks, Dick's, Bambino's
- **Queen Anne** (5-10 min walk): Toulouse Petit, Mecca Café
- **Belltown** (10-15 min walk): Dense restaurant cluster with 20+ options

## About

Created for visitors and staff of the Pacific Science Center to discover nearby food options within walking distance.
